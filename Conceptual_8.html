Details

Advertisement 1 - The story of artist Mr. Bones

Mr Bones was very sad and depressed because he could not paint colourful paintings. He tried again and again but at the end he had to throw them away. His wife really worried about Mr Bones and wanted to help him. She comforted Mr Bones: do not doubt your ability; maybe there is just something wrong with your pigments. I use Kiehl’s and it make my face looks colourful and shiny, maybe you can tryto mix Kiehl’s products with your pigment.Mr Bones mixes the Kiehl’s product with his pigment.

He started to paint again and when the moment the brush touched his painting; magic happened. The whole painting became colourful immediately. Mr Bourne was shocked and took a step back;he dropped a droplet of pigment by accident and at the time the pigment touched the ground, the pigment splashed colour to the entire room. At that moment, Mr Bones realised the power of Kiehl’s and he wanted to do something much more meaningful when he saw the black and white world.

Mr Bones quickly walked to the window and started to paint the world; Without doubt, the whole world became meaningful because of the Kiehl’s product. Mr Bourne turned back and started to paint the screen of in order to make audiences' life colourful. Finally our slogan showed up with the colourful background Mr Bones painted.
