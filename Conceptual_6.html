Photo Concert

Music to visual ~

When I closing my eyes and enjoying the melody and rhythm of Opening, an image of “a person keeps moving upwards and finally get to the heaven” comes to my mind. Therefore, I chose different types of stairways as the main object of this serious of photography. Also, the extreme angle looking up is implying the force of moving upwards.

Details

00:00-01:00

The melody and rhythm are light and smooth in this clip, which gives
me a feeling of a person moving with a clear aim and full power.
Therefore, the tone of this photo is light and it seems that the heaven
(where the stairway reaches the awning) is clear.

01:01-03:56

For this clip, the rhythm becomes faster and the melody is rapider,
which gives me a feeling of that person is rushing to the destination
anxiously, but the stairway seems never ends. Besides, the volume is
changeable which express that person’s struggle between “frustrated”
and “unwilling to give up”. Thus, the tone of this photo is gloomy.
Also, the stairs seem endless and the heaven (the roof decorations
and the sky) is so vague and far away.

03:57-06:03

This part’s music is lively. The rhythm speeds up gradually and the melody
is repetitive, which gives me a feeling of that person is start to moving
upward with a faith that I will finally reach the heaven (the Logo Square
awning and the sky) as long as I keep moving. Therefore, l captured fewer
stairs and the tone bright. The sunlight that penetrates the logo square
awning implies that the light is not far away. This is a photo that full of hope.

Represents the clip 06:03-06:24

With the rhythm slows down gently, I got the image of that person walk into
the heaven step by step. I feel like the long last sound represent that person ‘s
fulfilment of finally reaching the heaven and all the effort paying off. Thus,
everything in this photo is bright and clear. I only captured less than ten
stairs to imply the heaven is near at hand and I wish the blue sky is able to
represent the peaceful state of mind after that person reaching the heaven.
