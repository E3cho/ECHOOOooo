Set design

The first floor is really special as it changes a lot during the story. Before the room being decorate, it’s just a normal room, but after the decoration, it looks like a party room. So finally I decided to draw the room after it being decorate as it more related to the story. As you can see, a lot of party elements (e.g. balloon and banner) are added to make the overall mood warm and happy.

On the second floor, since I only need the bedroom setting, so I treat it as a part of a house. There are two bed which implying this a single-parent family which live a mother and her son. Also, the bed of the son is a bit messy, however, the single mother’s bed is pretty clean, which shows their personalities – son is very naughty and active while his mother love clean and tidy.

3-column Script

I focus more on the script than the visual image. The reason for using what kind of shot, how one shot connect with another, background sound and dialogue are all clearly written.

Model sheets of character design

Character 1 - The single mother
A women who is about 28 years old and around 1.65m tall. Wearing nightclothes since she is sleeping in the story a first. She care about her son pretty much, but also always mad at her son because her son is really naughty.

Character 2 - The son
His is an eight years old boy who is around 1.20m tall. Wearing school uniform since in the story, he is just after school. He is Very naughty and active, will always bring trouble to his mother

Written description

Today is the single mother’s birthday!

the son plan to celebrate his mother’s birthday and prepare a birthday surprise for his mother. Since he know normally his mother will sleeping during the time he after school, he comes back home and decorate the first floor secretly and plan to bring the cake to the second floor to surprise his mother and wake his mother up.

But he fell down by the stair and the cake crashed. The mother is awakened by the fell down sound too. The mother is really angry the his son even bring trouble to her on her birthday and she is keeping blaming her son. The son feel very wronged as he really spend a lot of time preparing the surprise but made her mother become angry. So, he climb out of the window.

The mother thought her son jump off a building, but that is actually part of the real surprise the son prepare for his mother, so dose the crushing cake and annoying his mother.
